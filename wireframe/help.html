Frequently Asked Questions

To create your first invoice:

  1. Navigate to Invoices > Create Invoice from the sidebar
  2. Select or add a customer
  3. Add line items for products or services
  4. Configure tax and discount if needed
  5. Review the total and click "Save & Send Invoice"

There are two ways to add a customer:

Method 1: Go to Customers > Add Customer and fill in the customer details form.

Method 2: When creating an invoice, click "Add New Customer" in the customer selection modal.

To track expenses:

  1. Go to Expenses > Add Expense
  2. Enter the expense amount and select a category
  3. Add vendor information and upload receipt if available
  4. Click "Save Expense" to record it

To manage user permissions:

  1. Navigate to Settings > User Management
  2. Click on a user to edit their details
  3. Adjust their role and specific permissions
  4. Click "Save Changes" to update

Note: Only administrators can manage user permissions.

To generate reports:

  1. Go to the Reports section in the sidebar
  2. Select the report type (Profit & Loss, Balance Sheet, Cash Flow, etc.)
  3. Choose the date range for the report
  4. Click "Generate Report" or use the export options

To customize invoice appearance:

  1. Go to Settings > Business Settings
  2. Navigate to the "Invoice Customization" tab
  3. Choose your primary color, logo, and footer text
  4. Click "Preview" to see how it looks
  5. Save your changes
